Getting Started:

Role a first dice.

You get your first points excluding 1.

You can choose to hold or continue.

After your first ponits. its accumulated.

If you role a dice with 1 you lose the point during the turn.

Reach 100 points to win the game.

If you role two dice and you role 1 you lose the points for the other dice turn and if the two dice turn all 1s you lose all points in the entire game.
